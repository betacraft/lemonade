Lemonades Beta Program

Lemonades beta program, is a validation phase for the next-step of online shopping in India. Before starting to serve the need of every online buyer, we want to test, refine and ease out the business model.

We are not using beta tag for getting the cushion for probable hiccups! We have invested our whole and sole to make this service as super efficient as possible.

For the initial few months, we will be manually moderating each of every member of the Lemonades community and every deal that is going on, so as to make sure the validity and authenticity of every bit of the information available on the platform.

At any point, if you are unsatisfied with the service, or find any unpleasant, untrue content on the site, we are just a tweet/a phone call/an email away from you :). We take 100% responsibility of every bit of content on the site.

Again I would like to say thank you for helping us out.
